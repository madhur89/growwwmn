Manoj Kumar Bansal

DIRECTOR

Grow Money Capital Pvt. Ltd. is led by the Chairman CA Manoj Kumar Bansal. He is responsible for the day-to-day management of the NBFC and for building the necessary scale to make Grow Money capital, a leader and a role model in the sectors in which it operates. In this role, he develops and implements robust business plans with a focus on building a strong team with a culture of high performance, transparency, and accountability.

Manoj Kumar Bansal is a part of several industry forums, including Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, was lending his expertise as a member of Regional Council- Northern India Banking and Financial Institutions Committee. He has over 22 years of experience in Finance & Consultancy. He is a Chartered Accountant from ICAI.

Gurmeet Singh Grewal

Independent Director

He specializes in advising on Company Law and Tax matters, conducting Financial Reviews, handling appeals before tax authorities and the Company Law Board. He has extensive experience converting single entry accounts to double entry. Additionally, he serve as the Secretary of the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT) Bar Association, New Delhi, and have held various roles within the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), including membership in the Financial Reporting and Review Board (FRRB) and participation in committees such as the Company Law Study Group on Schedule VI. Life Member of ITAT Bar Association, he was also part of the Executive Committee from 2002-03 to 2004-05.
